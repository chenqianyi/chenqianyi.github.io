personal review

Time flies, the turn of the high
one has passed, we ushered in the moment of summary. This year, for everyone, is
a new model and a new experience. During the school year, it is worth affirming
that I was able to better enter the international curriculum this mode of education,
the fastest way to adapt to the curriculum.

This year, because of the outbreak,
we have gone through a very special semester. The special experience of online classes
has made me understand that self-discipline and initiative are necessary and valuable
in an unattended learning state. Of course, in the process of continuous learning,
I also found some of my problems. For example, sometimes inefficient learning leads
to poor results. Evenings sometimes waste time so that you can’t review all the
things you’ve learned, leading some points of knowledge to be confused or even remembered
during exams. So improving the efficiency of learning is my next goal. However,
it is worth affirming that I in this year’s exam each course has achieved a relatively
good result, I hope that next year can be a higher level!

Of course, in the class activities,
I also did not fall. My experience as a publicity committee member this year has
taught me the importance of teamwork and the need to arrange my time well. Discussing
new ideas and advising your peers are all indispensable elements of the position.
Remember this Father’s Day board because of the approach to the final exam and tired
of coping, and finally used the afternoon to go to the school to print posters.
Of course my peers and I are doing their part, but there is still something wrong
with it. If next year still serve as a publicity committee, hope to be able to better
serve the class!

Grade 11 is coming, I hope I can
continue to work hard, forward!
